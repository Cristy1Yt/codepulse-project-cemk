Cookie Policy

At CodePulse S.R.L., your privacy and data security are top priorities. In line with our commitment to innovation and secure software development, we use cookies to provide a better experience on our website and ensure the functionality, performance, and safety of our digital services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how you interact with our content, remember your preferences, and improve the overall user experience.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • Ensure core website functionality
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Enhance security and performance
  • Support marketing and service promotion
  • Personalize your experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose
Necessary Cookies Essential for website operation, including navigation and security.
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how users interact with the website (e.g., Google Analytics).
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and enhance functionality.
Marketing Cookies Support our promotion efforts via partners (e.g., Devopcity).

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, PortSwigger tools) that place cookies on your device. These tools help us monitor the quality, performance, and cybersecurity posture of our services.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings or through our website's cookie preferences panel. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact site functionality.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We encourage you to revisit this page periodically.
